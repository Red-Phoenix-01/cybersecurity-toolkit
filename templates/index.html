Cybersecurity Toolkit 🔐

🧠 What Each Tool Does:

📩 Phishing Email Detector

Detects common scam phrases used in phishing emails, such as "verify your account", "click this link", or "login urgently" to help protect users from email fraud.

🌐 Malicious URL Scanner

Checks URLs for suspicious patterns like shortening services (e.g., bit.ly), misleading domains, or words like "free", "secure", "verify" that are often used in malicious links.

🛠 Vulnerability Port Scanner

Scans a domain or IP address for commonly open ports like 21, 22, 80, 443 to identify services that might be vulnerable or exposed to the internet.
