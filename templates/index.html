🧠 What Each Tool Does

🌐 Malicious URL Scanner
Analyzes URLs for suspicious patterns like shortening services (bit.ly), fake login domains, uncommon ports, encoded payloads (like Base64), and typosquatting. Also integrates with VirusTotal to check live threat intelligence from 70+ security engines.

📩 Phishing Email Detector
Detects scam-like language used in phishing emails such as “verify your account”, “click this link”, or “login urgently”. Flags red-flag phrases to help identify suspicious content.

🛠 Vulnerability Port Scanner
Scans an IP address or domain for commonly exposed ports (21, 22, 80, 443, etc.) to simulate basic vulnerability reconnaissance — helps detect potentially open services on a target.
